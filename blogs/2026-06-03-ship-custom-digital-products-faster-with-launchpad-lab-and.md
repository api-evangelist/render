Render has announced a partnership with LaunchPad Lab, a Chicago-based digital product consultancy with 12+ years of experience, over 240 companies served, and a 4.8/5 Clutch rating. The collaboration combines LaunchPad Lab's product strategy and custom development expertise with Render's cloud infrastructure to help teams accelerate time-to-market and reduce infrastructure overhead. Clients deploying on Render gain access to both the consultancy's strategic guidance and a platform trusted by millions of developers, suited for traditional applications and AI-native products alike.
